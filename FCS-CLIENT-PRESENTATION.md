### 2. NEW Market Opportunity: Condo Remediation

**Major finding:** Florida's SB 4-D (Surfside Law) is creating a $500M-$2B remediation market in Tampa Bay alone.

**What we built:**
- 3 new pillar pages targeting this emerging market
- `/condo-milestone-inspection-remediation/`
- `/sb-4-d-compliance-tampa-bay-condos/`
- `/condo-structural-reserve-study-repairs/`

**Why this matters:**
- 900,000+ Florida condo units now require mandatory inspections
- Deadlines: Dec 31, 2025 (SIRS), Jan 1, 2026 (reserve funding)
- Condo boards are actively searching for remediation contractors
- **No competitors have Tampa-specific remediation content**
