# FCS AI Image Prompts v2 - Refined

Based on competitor analysis and QA feedback.

## Key Learnings
1. **Avoid people in foreground** - proportions are hard to get right
2. **Show completed/near-complete work** - cleaner, more professional
3. **Simpler compositions** - less clutter
4. **If people needed**: show from behind, silhouette, or far distance
5. **Focus on the work, not the workers**
